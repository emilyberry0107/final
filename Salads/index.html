STRAWBERRY PRETZEL SALAD

3 tbsp. Sugar

1 1/2 Stick Margarine

1 1/2 c. Pretzels

8 oz. Cream Cheese

6 oz. Strawberry Jell-o

8 oz. Cool Whip

2-10 oz. Frozen Strawberries

Instructions

Cream 3T sugar and margarine and add pretzels.

Press in bottom of 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 for 10 min. Cool.

Combine 1 c suagr and cream cheese until smooth.

Add Cool Whip. Pour in crust.

Mix Jell-o in 2c boiling water. Add 1/2c cool water. Add strawberries. Let set up until firm. Pour over cream cheese filling. Refrigerate.
