Desserts

QUICK TURTLES

1 1/2 c. Pecan Halves

5 Bars 1 1/2 oz. Milk Chocolate

1 pkg. 14 oz. Caramels

Instructions

Arrange pecans in clusters of 3 or 4 halves 2 in. apart on greased sheet.

Top each cluster with one caramel slightly flattened.

Bake at 300 for 7 minutes or until caramels are soften.

Remove to wax paper.

Flatten with spatula and top each with one bar of milk chocolate while warm.

Refridgerate until harden
