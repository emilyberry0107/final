COOL LIME DRESSING

1 Lime

1 Container (8oz) Key Lime Yogurt

1 Cup Cool Whip

Toasted Coconut

Instructions

Zest and juice the lime.

Combine yogurt, 1 tsp. zest, 1 tbs. juice, & cool whip. Whisk until smoth

Pour over fresh fruit. Top with toasted coconut.

For a thicker fruit dip, use all the juice from the lime and the entire carton of cool whip.
