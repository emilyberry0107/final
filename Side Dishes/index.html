MAPLE APPLE RINGS

3 Large Apples (peeled, cored, & slced into 1/4 inch rings)

3/4 c. All Purpose Flour

1/4 c. Maple Syrup

1 Egg (beaten)

1/4 c. Butter Milk

Powdered Sugar

Oil for deep fat frying

Instructions

In a shallow bowl, combine the Flour, Egg, Syrup, & Butter Milk.

Dip Apple Rings on both sides into batter.

In an electric skillet or deep fat fryer, heat 2 inches of Oil to 375.

Fry apple rings, a few at a time for 2 minutes or until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.

Dust with Powdered Sugar
